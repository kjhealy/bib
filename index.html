philbib

BibTeX file of philosophy papers and books. The master file contains about 8,000 entries.

Fork of PhilGeek's (Mark Kalderon's) bib repo, a bibtex file of philosophy books and articles. This fork merges in (and sorts) Ted Sider's philosophy bibfile and Andrew Cullison's compilations of the contents of several major philosophy journals.

Authors and Indirect Contributors

Mark Eli Kalderon.
Ted Sider.
Andrew Cullison.
Kieran Healy.

Contact

Kieran Healy is responsible for pulling the files together and merging them. The other people did all the actual work of assembling the bibliographies.
